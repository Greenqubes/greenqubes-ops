### NotificationDrawer — decoupled from jobs prop

Previously `NotificationDrawer` required `jobs: ScheduleJob[]` to be passed in from the parent shell (for overdue job detection). This made it impossible to use on pages that don't fetch the full job list.

**Fix:** Removed the `jobs` prop entirely. The component now:
1. Fetches its own overdue jobs on mount + on drawer open via Supabase client
2. Queries `jobs` table for `status = 'scheduled'` and `date <= today`, then filters for time-based overdue in JS
3. Uses `as unknown as` type cast (consistent with rest of codebase)

**Files:**
- `src/features/notifications/NotificationDrawer.tsx`

### BottomNav — kept on list/dashboard pages only

Initial plan was to add BottomNav to job form shells too. After preview review, removed from JobDetailShell and NewJobShell — the job forms have their own fixed action bar (Save / Cancel / Push for Approval etc.) and adding BottomNav below it felt cramped and redundant.

BottomNav remains on: Schedule, Approvals, Installer, Assistant, Admin.

---

### AdminShell header stacking fix

AdminShell has its own bespoke sticky header (back arrow + "Admin" title + tab strip). After adding CompanyBar above it, both were `sticky top-0` and overlapped. Fixed by changing AdminShell's header from `sticky top-0 z-40` to `sticky top-[45px] z-20`.

**File:** `src/features/admin/AdminShell.tsx`

## Key decisions

- **CompanyBar owns its own sticky/z-index** — callers just drop `<CompanyBar lang={lang} />` with no wrapper needed.
- **lang defaults to 'en'** — safe fallback for shells that might not have it easily available.
- **BottomNav excluded from job forms** — confirmed by Nic after preview review. Job forms have their own action bar; the nav would be redundant and cramped.
- **NotificationDrawer self-fetches overdue** — cleaner separation of concerns; bell works correctly on every page regardless of whether the parent fetches jobs.
