sales
Creating a job
1
Fill in job details
Project title, client company, contact person, date, time, location, description, and production instructions. AI can suggest wording for any field.
2
Attach files
Upload permits, BCA documents, designer JOs, or any other files into named buckets. Add URLs for external documents.
3
Push for Approval
System checks for installer clashes automatically. If all clear, job goes to the scheduler's approvals queue. Sales gets a Telegram confirmation.
4
Track status
Job moves from Pending → Awaiting Approval → Scheduled. Sales can recall and edit at any point before the scheduler approves.
scheduler
Approving & scheduling
1
Review the approval queue
All pending submissions appear with job details, client, and the sales person who created it. Badge shows the count.
2
Check team workload
Interactive workload chart shows how loaded each installer is for the week. Navigate forward and back to pick the best fit.
3
Approve & Notify — or Send Back
One tap approves the job, adds it to the schedule, and sends a Telegram to the assigned installers. Or send back to sales with a written reason.
4
Monitor the live schedule
Calendar, week, and list views with status colour-coding. Filter by installer, status, or date range.
installer
On the job
1
Receive Telegram notification
When the scheduler approves a job, the assigned installers get an instant Telegram message with the date, time, location, and a link to the job.
2
Sign in and view the job
Personal Gmail works. Installers see only their jobs: Today, Up Next, This Week. Job details, production instructions, and all attached files are right there.
3
Chat with the team
WhatsApp-style job chat with voice notes and photo capture. All messages stay attached to the job — visible to sales and scheduler too.
4
Upload completion photos
Photos are required before marking done. Scheduler can override if needed. All photos stored securely — no WhatsApp albums to dig through.
Beyond the basics
Built-in intelligence
🤖
AI Assistant
Ask anything — job history, client preferences, supplier contacts, company SOPs. The assistant has web search and remembers past conversations. The more the team uses it, the smarter it gets. A Monday digest surfaces the most important conversations for the team's knowledge base.
⚡ Clash Detection
Catches double-booked installers the moment sales tries to submit. Shows alternatives and lets you shift the time or swap the installer — all in the same screen.
📲 Telegram Notifications
Job assigned, approved, sent back, or overdue — the right person gets the right message. No manual follow-ups. Notifications link directly to the job in the app.
🌐 Three Languages
Full English, Simplified Chinese, and Bengali UI. Each user picks their preferred language — all date and day labels stay in English.
🌙 Dark Mode
Full dark theme available on all devices. Auto-detects system preference. Toggle from the user menu anytime.

Cost & infrastructure
Built to stay lean
🌐 Hosting — Vercel
Serverless. Auto-scales. Preview deployments for every change before it goes live.
🗄 Database — Supabase
Postgres with built-in auth, real-time sync, and role-level security. Data never leaves the DB without permission checks.
📦 File Storage — Cloudflare R2
Zero egress fees — installers downloading photos on mobile data costs nothing extra.
Monthly cost (current team)
~S$48–84
All-in: hosting, database, AI, file storage, image processing.
📈 If the team triples
Architecture doesn't change. Cost moves to ~S$145–210/mo — still cheaper than a single SaaS licence per person.
🔒 Security
Row-level security on every table. Installers cannot see pricing. No sensitive data in AI context. Google OAuth only.
